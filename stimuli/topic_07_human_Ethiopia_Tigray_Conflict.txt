TOPIC 7: Ethiopia / Tigray Conflict
============================================================
Issuing body: Lord Ahmad of Wimbledon, UK Minister for Human Rights
Date:         2 July 2021
Source URL:   https://www.gov.uk/government/speeches/it-is-time-to-put-the-interests-of-ethiopian-people-first
Word count:   50

--- HUMAN-AUTHORED TEXT (verbatim) ---

All sides – the Federal Government of Ethiopia, Tigray Defence Forces, Amhara militias and Eritrean Defence Forces – have an opportunity to end the cycle of violence and suffering. We urge them to take it. And we call on Eritrean forces to withdraw, as requested by the Ethiopian government.
