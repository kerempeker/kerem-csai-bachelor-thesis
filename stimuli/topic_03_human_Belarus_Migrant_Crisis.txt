TOPIC 3: Belarus Migrant Crisis
============================================================
Issuing body: G7 Foreign Ministers (Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, UK, US) and EU High Representative
Date:         November 2021
Source URL:   https://www.gov.uk/government/news/belarus-g7-foreign-ministers-statement-on-migrant-crisis
Word count:   59

--- HUMAN-AUTHORED TEXT (verbatim) ---

We, the G7 Foreign Ministers of Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan, the United Kingdom and the United States of America, and the High Representative of the European Union, condemn the Belarus regime's orchestration of irregular migration across its borders. These callous acts are putting people's lives at risk and we call on Belarus to cease this activity immediately.
