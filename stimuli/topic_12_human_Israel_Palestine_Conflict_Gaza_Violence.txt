TOPIC 12: Israel–Palestine Conflict / Gaza Violence
============================================================
Issuing body: Ambassador Barbara Woodward, UK Permanent Representative to the UN
Date:         30 August 2021
Source URL:   https://www.gov.uk/government/speeches/preventing-further-escalation-of-violence-in-israel-and-the-occupied-palestinian-territories
Word count:   60

--- HUMAN-AUTHORED TEXT (verbatim) ---

We share concerns about the recent tensions in Gaza and call on all parties to take steps to avoid exacerbating tensions and to maintain the cessation of hostilities. The United Kingdom condemns unequivocally Hamas' indiscriminate attacks against Israel, including the use of incendiary balloons. We call upon Hamas and other terrorist groups to permanently end their rocket fire against Israel.
