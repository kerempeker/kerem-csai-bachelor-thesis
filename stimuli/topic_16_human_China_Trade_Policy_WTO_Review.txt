TOPIC 16: China Trade Policy / WTO Review
============================================================
Issuing body: Simon Manley, UK Ambassador to the WTO and UN in Geneva
Date:         20 October 2021
Source URL:   https://www.gov.uk/government/speeches/wto-trade-policy-review-of-china-uk-statement
Word count:   59

--- HUMAN-AUTHORED TEXT (verbatim) ---

We recognise the recent progress China has made to open its markets to international trade and investment. We welcome China's engagement with plurilateral initiatives such as the JSI on investment facilitation, E-Commerce and domestic regulation, as well as the Trade and Health Initiative, the Informal Dialogue on Plastics Pollution and Environmentally Sustainable Plastics - all good initiatives.
