TOPIC 18: International Criminal Court — 20th Anniversary
============================================================
Issuing body: Ambassador James Kariuki CMG, UK Deputy Permanent Representative to the United Nations
Date:         24 June 2022
Source URL:   https://www.gov.uk/government/speeches/reflecting-on-our-relationship-with-the-international-criminal-court-after-20-years-of-the-rome-statute
Word count:   79

--- HUMAN-AUTHORED TEXT (verbatim) ---

We note the obligation of States Parties to cooperate with the Court under the Rome Statute, and we also call on all States to cooperate with the Court where there are UN Security Council Resolutions which require this. We will continue to demonstrate our support for the Court, and to work together with States Parties and the Court, to ensure that the Court delivers justice for victims, and accountability, in respect of the most serious crimes of international concern.
