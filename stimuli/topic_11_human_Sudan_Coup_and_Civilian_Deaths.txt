TOPIC 11: Sudan Coup and Civilian Deaths
============================================================
Issuing body: Joint Statement (UK, US, EU, Norway, Switzerland)
Date:         8 June 2022
Source URL:   https://www.gov.uk/government/news/joint-statement-on-death-toll-since-25th-military-coup-in-sudan
Word count:   55

--- HUMAN-AUTHORED TEXT (verbatim) ---

We deeply regret the loss of Sudanese lives, killed in large-scale attacks and violence across the country during the same period. The loss of life and many injured represent a heavy toll for the people of Sudan. We would like to extend our sympathies to the families and friends of the victims.
