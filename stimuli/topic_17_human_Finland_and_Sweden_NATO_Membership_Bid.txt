TOPIC 17: Finland and Sweden NATO Membership Bid
============================================================
Issuing body: Rt Hon Elizabeth Truss MP, Secretary of State for Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Affairs
Date:         16 May 2022
Source URL:   https://www.gov.uk/government/news/uk-welcomes-sweden-and-finlands-nato-membership-plans
Word count:   54

--- HUMAN-AUTHORED TEXT (verbatim) ---

We look forward to working with them as new NATO Allies and stand ready to offer them our every assistance during the accession process. Our mutual security declarations signed with Sweden and Finland last week by the Prime Minister demonstrate our steadfast and unequivocal commitment to both countries during this process and beyond.
